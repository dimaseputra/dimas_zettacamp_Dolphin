My Pokémon Starter

Pokémon are mysterious creatures with many secrets. Some Pokémon live with humans and some live outdoors in meadows, caves, or the sea, but much about their ecology is still unknown.

Pikachu

Pikachu is a small, yellow electric rodent Pokémon known for its adorable appearance and lightning bolt-shaped tail. With its big, round eyes and rosy cheeks, Pikachu exudes a mix of cuteness and energy.

Mudkip Shiny

Jolteon is a swift and electrifying Pokémon with a yellow, spiky-furred body. It harnesses lightning-like speed and can unleash potent electric shocks from its fur, making it a fierce and dynamic Eeveelution.

Ditto

Ditto is a unique, pink, gelatinous Pokémon known for its transformative abilities. Its malleable body can take on the exact appearance and characteristics of any other Pokémon, making it a master of disguise.

Jolteon

Jolteon is an electrifying Pokémon with sleek, spiky fur and a striking, yellow coloration. Evolved from Eevee through exposure to an Electric Stone, Jolteon possesses lightning-fast agility and the power to discharge intense electric shocks from its fur.
